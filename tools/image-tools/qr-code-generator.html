About QR Codes

QR (Quick Response) codes are two-dimensional barcodes that can be scanned using a smartphone camera or QR code reader app. They can store various types of information, such as:

  • URLs: Link to websites or specific web pages
  • Text: Store plain text information
  • Contact Information: Share contact details in vCard format
  • Email: Open an email client with pre-filled address, subject, and body
  • Phone Numbers: Initiate phone calls
  • SMS: Open a text message with pre-filled number and message
  • WiFi Network: Connect to WiFi networks automatically
QR Code Error Correction

QR codes include error correction capabilities, allowing them to be read even if parts of the code are damaged or obscured. There are four levels of error correction:

  • Low (L): 7% of codewords can be restored
  • Medium (M): 15% of codewords can be restored
  • Quartile (Q): 25% of codewords can be restored
  • High (H): 30% of codewords can be restored

Higher error correction levels make the QR code more robust but also increase its complexity and size.
