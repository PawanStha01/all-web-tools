About Percentage Calculations

Percentages are a way of expressing a number as a fraction of 100. They are commonly used in finance, statistics, and everyday calculations. Here's how different percentage calculations work:

Percentage of a Number

To find X% of Y:

  • Convert the percentage to a decimal: X ÷ 100
  • Multiply the decimal by Y
  • Example: 15% of 80 = 0.15 × 80 = 12
Percentage Change

To find the percentage change from an original value to a new value:

  • Subtract the original value from the new value
  • Divide the result by the original value
  • Multiply by 100 to get the percentage
  • Example: Change from 50 to 60 = ((60 - 50) ÷ 50) × 100 = 20% increase
Percentage Difference

To find the percentage difference between two values:

  • Find the absolute difference between the two values
  • Divide by the average of the two values
  • Multiply by 100 to get the percentage
  • Example: Difference between 50 and 60 = (|60 - 50| ÷ ((50 + 60) ÷ 2)) × 100 = 18.18%
Discount Calculation

To find the discounted price:

  • Calculate the discount amount: Original Price × (Discount Percentage ÷ 100)
  • Subtract the discount amount from the original price
  • Example: 20% off $100 = $100 - ($100 × 0.2) = $80
