About Word Counter

Our Word Counter tool provides a comprehensive analysis of your text. It counts:

  • Words: Total number of words in your text.
  • Characters (with spaces): Total number of characters including spaces.
  • Characters (without spaces): Total number of characters excluding spaces.
  • Sentences: Total number of sentences (text ending with period, exclamation mark, or question mark).
  • Paragraphs: Total number of paragraphs (text separated by line breaks).
  • Reading Time: Estimated time to read the text (based on average reading speed of 225 words per minute).

This tool is perfect for students, writers, content creators, and social media managers who need to keep track of their word count for essays, articles, blog posts, or social media captions.
